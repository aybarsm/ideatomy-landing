Hi, we are Ideatomy!

Your next-generation idea provider. Yeah, you read it right. Ideatomy is the combination of words structured from idea and anatomy. As Wiki says, and we quote, “Anatomy is the branch of biology concerned with the study of the structure of organisms and their parts.”. So, Ideatomy is a digital company that concerns the structure and the parts of brilliant ideas. In other words, we produce and vitalise brilliant ideas by evaluating their design and internal workings.

Who are we?

We are a team that developed many infrastructure, platform, and software as a service projects with years of experience.

First of all, apart from our work, we read a lot. We constantly follow the global information technologies market to seek new opportunities to build brilliant in-house projects. As a think-tank provider, we also contribute, consult, develop and deploy collaborative projects. Since our team has years of experience even with infrastructure as a service layer, we can provide budget-friendly and shortcut solutions or technologies to vitalise your projects in a short time. Overall, Ideatomy is all about inspecting thoroughly and generating new perspectives accordingly.
